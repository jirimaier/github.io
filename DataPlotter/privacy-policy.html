Privacy Policy for Data Plotter

Introduction

Data Plotter is a desktop application developed by Jiří Maier as an individual from Czechia. This privacy policy explains how Data Plotter handles user data (or the lack thereof) and outlines your rights as a user.

Data Collection

Data Plotter does not collect any personal information from its users. It operates entirely offline and does not communicate with external servers or databases.

Cookies and Tracking

Data Plotter does not use cookies or any tracking technologies.
